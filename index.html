Introducing the Mistborn Box

The first-ever licensed box of the Mistborn series by Brandon Sanderson. Coming to you straight from the plains of Scadrial, a planet much like our own, filled with stories that will grab you tight and shake you to the core. Carefully designed to capture the essence of the epic novels and represent the meticulous world of magic, tyranny, and a total dose of excitement, this box has been marked with the Steel Alphabet known to this world, a set of glyphs used to represent various metals and numbers.
